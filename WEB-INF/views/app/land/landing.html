Analytics CONTINUUM

Flashy charts, gauges, and pretty dashboards with all the swanky features of drill-downs, filters and the what-nots are great but then, Dashboards are only visualizations of the Data. Adding intelligence and Analytics is what differentiates the Verita BI platform from a dashboard.

Verita presents the Analytics Continuum of Descriptive, Diagnostic, Predictive and Prescriptive Analytics to help define what happened? Why did it happen? What will happen? And What should I do?

BLUESWAN SUITE

The BlueSwan suite comprises five elements, specifically designed to accelerate and assure quality to your processes.

Prudentia

Prudentia is Cigniti's model-based testing tool that automatically generates software testing procedures using models of systems requirements and behavior.

Velocita

Test automation with assured return on investment is the key. Cigniti's Velocita ensures that this becomes a reality.

Cesta

Cesta is the world's first integrated test asset migration platform, consisting of QuickLean, Migrate2Selenium, and M2TC.

Praxia

Praxia is a process accelerator that helps in transforming our clients' QA and Testing practices by assessing Processes, Practices, Tools, Skills, and Risks.

Verita

Verita comes with the ability to analyze data and provide data from descriptive, diagnostic, predictive, and prescriptive viewpoints.

VERITA

Cigniti's BlueSwan Verita is a BI Platform that pans across the enterprise to provide insights and Analytics with data catering to the needs of diverse stakeholders.

Verita's holistic approach is to realise the Enterprise Vision with accelerated Velocity without trade-off in Quality.

It is a role based Dashboard/BI Platform which is tool agnostic. It has the ability to provide Mashup dashboards in real time which can be viewed on the go using Phones, Tablets and the likes due to its Responsive design. It is a non-intrusive and light-weight solution that is both highly-scalable and has superlative performance.
